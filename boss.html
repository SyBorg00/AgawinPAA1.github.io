Bosses

Bosses are the main challenges that the Terrarians must go through should they want to progress further into the game. Depending on the gamemode, each boss will have various healthbars and damage, and will have modified attack patterns. Each bosses can either be summoned by natural means, or having to summon them via certin summoning tools. Keep in mind that each boss will have specific summoning condition, so it is best for you to always be prepared before facing them one-by-one
