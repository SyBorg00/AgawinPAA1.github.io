NPCS

NPCs are friendly entities within the game that can help aid in you in your exploration progress. Some of them can provide useful tips on how to be efficient in the game. While others can provide you with useful items in exchange of some money. Each NPCs, with the exception of some, require a home to be fully present within your world. So make sure that you can build vacant houses for each of the NPCs to appear. Additionally, each of the NPCs will spawn when certain conditions are met, like defeating a boss, having possession of certain items, etc. And finally in the latest verion, each NPC will have a different 'happiness factor' depending on what biome they are currently living in. So take note of that as well.




This page will contain a tidbit information of every NPCs that the player can acquire, and ways on how to obtain each one of them.
